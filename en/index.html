The Property

A living heritage
in the heart of Menorca

Finca S'Espinal is an exclusive 30,000 m² agricultural estate in Sant Climent, minutes from Mahón. A traditional two-storey Menorcan villa, private pool, high-quality orchards, private well and 15 outbuildings make this one of the most unique opportunities in the Balearic real estate market.

Currently in the process of plot subdivision, offering complete privacy, water self-sufficiency and a privileged rural setting within the UNESCO Biosphere Reserve.
